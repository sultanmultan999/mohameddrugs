How it works

This star rating system allows for precise ratings with half-star increments:

  • Click on the right side of a star to give a full star
  • Click on the left side of a star to give a half star
  • For example: Click left side of 3rd star = 2.5 rating
  • Click right side of 4th star = 4.0 rating

Try it out! Hover over stars to preview, click to set your rating.
